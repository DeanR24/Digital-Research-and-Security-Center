Adding an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA helps prevent unauthorized access even if your password is compromised.

üì° Secure Your Wi-Fi

Ensure your home Wi-Fi network is secured by:

  • Using a strong, unique password.
  • Have a guest network.
  • Create a separate network for IoT devices like Alexa and smart bulbs to enhance security and isolate them from sensitive data.
  • Replace your router every 4-5 years or until outdated.
  • Keep track of devices that are connected to home network.
  • Enabling WPA3 or WPA2 encryption.
  • Turning off WPS to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • Regularly updating your router‚Äôs firmware.

üè† Enhance Physical Security

Protect your devices and data by:

  • Locking your devices when not in use.
  • Using privacy screens in public spaces.
  • Keeping sensitive documents secure.
  • Shredding confidential information before disposal.
  • Be cautious with USB devices and avoid using unknown or untrusted ones.
  • Use RFID-blocking wallets to protect credit cards and IDs from wireless theft.
